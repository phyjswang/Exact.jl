# Exact.jl

Provide exact solutions for benchmarking in algorithm development.

# Models

- Transverse field Ising chain
  - Thermodynamic limit
    - Ground state energy
    - Helmholtz free energy
    - Internal energy
    - Loschmidt rate function

- XXZ chain
  - Thermodynamic limit
    - Ground state energy

- Transverse field XY chain
  - Thermodynamic limit
    - Ground state energy

- Classical Ising model on the square/kagome/triangular lattice
  - Thermodynamic limit
    - Helmholtz free energy
